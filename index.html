About Epic Kids

Professional childcare rooted in care, consistency and community

Epic Kids Ltd was established to give families a childcare setting they can trust completely — where children are known by name, supported individually and encouraged to explore with confidence.

Our qualified practitioners follow the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS), combining structured activities with free play, outdoor time and meaningful conversations that build language, social skills and independence.

  • DBS-checked, paediatric first-aid trained staff
  • Secure entry, CCTV in communal areas and robust safeguarding policies
  • Nutritious meals and allergy-aware menus
  • Daily updates for parents via our communication app

Age groups

Rooms designed for each stage of early development

0–2 years

Baby Nest

A calm, sensory-rich space with gentle routines, tummy time, music and one-to-one bonding with key workers.

  • 1:3 staff ratio
  • Individual sleep & feeding plans
  • Soft-play & sensory exploration
2–3 years

Toddler Grove

Active learners build language, sharing skills and independence through guided play, stories and creative projects.

  • 1:4 staff ratio
  • Potty-training support
  • Small-group learning circles
3–5 years

Pre-School Peak

School-readiness focus — phonics foundations, numeracy games, teamwork and confidence for the next big step.

  • 1:8 staff ratio
  • School transition programme
  • STEM & arts workshops

For parents

Transparency you can rely on

Choosing childcare is one of the biggest decisions a family makes. We make it easier with clear policies, welcoming staff and a settling-in process tailored to your child.

Settling in

Gradual visits over 1–2 weeks so your child builds trust at their own pace.

Fees & funding

Competitive daily rates with guidance on 15 & 30-hour funded childcare eligibility.

What to bring

Spare clothes, comforters, nappies and any medication with completed consent forms.

Policies

Safeguarding, inclusion, behaviour and complaints policies available on request.
